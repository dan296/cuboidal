How To Play

Solve the cube with the least amount of swaps and rotations!

Incorrect Position

Partially Correct Position

Correct Position

  • Each edge of the cube makes up a five letter word.
  • Each face of the cube has 4 words that are connected by their first and last letters.
  • A cube is partially correct if it is on the correct edge or if it's in the correct spot with the incorrect rotation.
  • The limit for swaps and rotations is 60.

To rotate a cube, click the cube.

To swap 2 cubes, click and drag one cube onto another.
